Īdolia

The natural peoples of the Realms. They are thoughts, ideas, and beliefs made manifest — conceptually akin to thoughtforms, or perhaps, servitors without servitude.

While most Īdolia are human-like in size, shape, and temperment, some take on far more abstract forms. Celestial bodies, personifications of concepts, animated objects, and resident spirits are found throughout the Realms.

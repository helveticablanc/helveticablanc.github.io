Tattoo Policy

I get a lot of requests asking to either have my existing art tattooed on them, or to commission work specifically for a tattoo. I don't currently fulfill tattoo commissions for two reasons:

  1. I don't know the limitations of the medium
  2. I don't know the specialties of your tattoo artist

That said, my work is licensed under Creative Commons, so you have my blessing to use my art for personal tattoo reference.

If you are looking for a way to compensate me for its use, I highly recommend purchasing a print to bring to your tattoo artist as reference. It'll support my work, and give your artist something larger and easier to work from than an image on your phone.

Thank you, and happy inking!

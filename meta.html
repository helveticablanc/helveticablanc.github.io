The current version of this website was launched in April of 2022 and is hosted on GitHub. Written in plain HTML and CSS, this site contains no JavaScript, cookies, or trackers.

The overall design and digital garden concept was inspired by Devine Lu Linvega and other folks on Merveilles.

The title font, Basteleur Blanc, is a custom variant of Basteleur by Keussel of Velvetyne Type Foundry.

My eternal thanks to Taylor Stanley, Hannah Sullivan Facknitz, Aleks the Scrivener, Alex Melin, and Josaleigh Pollett for their eyes, ears, words, and support while I figure this whole thing out.

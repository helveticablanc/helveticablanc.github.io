Artwork

My work begins with techniques borrowed from mystics, magicians, occultists, and surreal artists. These include automatic drawing, asemic text, scrying, sigilization, divination, and more.

These methods are filtered through an ethos of sustainability, permacomputing, and an embrace of the ephemeral and imperfect.

Together, they help me explore my own complex feelings around spirituality, queerness, the culture around me, and the relationship between magic and art. All of this is then expressed through prints, cards, zines, and narratives filled with recurring characters, symbols, and thoughtforms.

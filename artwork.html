Artwork

My work is often a combination of traditional and digital mediums, but every piece starts with techniques borrowed from mystics, occultists, and surreal artists. These include automatic drawing, asemic text, scrying, sigilization, and divination.

These methods help me explore my own complex feelings around spirituality and religious trauma, gender and queerness, and my relationship to the culture that I was born into. All of this culminates in an ongoing worldbuilding project, where I fill Realms with characters, symbols, and thoughtforms, and you'll see these elements recur in my work frequently.

Artwork

My work begins with techniques borrowed from mystics, magicians, occultists, and surreal artists. These include automatic drawing, asemic text, scrying, sigilization, divination, and more.

These methods are filtered through an ethos of sustainability, permacomputing, and an embrace of the ephemeral and imperfect.

Together, they help me explore my own complex feelings around spirituality, religious trauma, gender, queerness, and my relationship to the culture around me. All of this is expressed through visual art, where I fill Realms with characters, symbols, and thoughtforms, all of which recur frequently in my work.

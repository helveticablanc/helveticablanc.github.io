Zines

While my art is generally published as single pieces meant for prints, zines are my way of gathering those pieces into collections. I have two regularly-published zines:

  • HOLY! — A monthly digital zine that collects all of my published artwork and sketches from Patreon into a nice, neat format.
  • Eidolia — A semi-annual physical and digital zine which is a collection of art experiments, usually revolving around a theme. Previous issues have been illustrations for machine-generated poetry, a guide to Wormrōte, a coloring book, sigils for magical New Years resolutions, and more!

I also occasionally publish standalone zines, such as Blanc—Chain™ — a postcrypto zine, or Everyday Sigils — a collection of tongue-in-cheek chaos magic sigils. All of my currently published zines can be found on itch.io, and all are listed below.

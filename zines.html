Zines

While my art is generally published as prints or cards, zines are my way of gathering those pieces into collections. I have two ongoing zines:

HOLY!

A quarterly digital zine that collects all of my published artwork and most sketches from Patreon into a nice, neat format.

Eidolia

An irregularly-published physical and digital zine which is a collection of art experiments, usually revolving around a theme. Some previous themes include:

  1. A guide to Wormrōte
  2. A coloring book
  3. Sigils for magical New Years resolutions

Other Zines

I also occasionally publish standalone zines, such as:

  1. Blanc—Chain™, a postcrypto zine
  2. Everyday Sigils, a collection of tongue-in-cheek chaos magic sigils.

All of my currently published zines are listed below and can be found on Itch.io.

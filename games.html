Games

From reading bones and dice to tarot cards, the connection between games, divination, and self-exploration is nearly as old as humanity itself. My goal is to make a series of games that can act as divination and self-exploration tools, starting with an oracle deck.

Oracle decks are a loose form of cartomancy, eschewing rules and tradition in favor of unique suits and more fluid, personalized interpretations. The archetypes aren't rigid, and thus, it's a fantastic place to experiment with form and meaning.

I'm currently working on an oracle deck, and you can follow along as I progress on Patreon. I'm also releasing a monthly series of exclusive, experimental collector's cards for patrons that can act as "expansion cards" for the main deck, or stand alone as small art pieces.
